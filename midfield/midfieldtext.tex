\chapter*{Mid-field probes of dark matter ($0.05\lesssim z \lesssim 2$)}

Major goals and themes:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Measure SMHM relation down to galaxies of mass YYY, to measure the halo mass function, and to constrain galaxy formation physics (necessary for CDM and non-CDM + baryon sims).
	\item Measure density profiles ( (SIDM and CDM) + baryons constraints)
	\item Satellites as probes of DM physics as a function of environment
	\item Proposed theory/sim program that grows in parallel with observational capabilities.
	\item Synergies w/Roman, Euclid, Rubin, etc.
\end{itemize}
